Medjumbe Island

Your Island Dreaming!

Medjumbe is a small private island, part of the protected Quirimbas Archipelago that stretches along Mozambique’s northern coastline. Here, the horizon is an endless swirl of white sands, turquoise lagoon and Indian Ocean blue. And with only 12 beach villas dotting our island, cast away in luxury Robinson Crusoe style.Set sail in search of whales and dolphins. Dive some of the world’s best reefs. Laze on deserted stretches of beach as sea turtles come to shore to lay their eggs.

At a glimpse

  • A private island with only 12 beach pool villas
  • Reached by all-inclusive helicopter transfer from Pemba
  • World-class diving, sailing, deep-sea fishing and water sports
  • Whale and dolphin watching
  • Island hopping
